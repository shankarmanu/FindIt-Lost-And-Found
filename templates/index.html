Platform Features

Everything you need
to recover what's yours

Our platform combines powerful technology with user-friendly design for the most effective lost and found solution.

📷

Photo Upload System

Attach clear images of lost or found items for visual matching. Multiple photos can be uploaded to show different angles and details, dramatically improving match accuracy.

🤖

Intelligent Matching

Automated system identifies similar items and connects owners with finders using advanced algorithms and keyword matching for near-instant results.

🗺️

Map Integration

Tag exact locations where items were lost or found for better tracking and geographical matching. Visual map view makes it easy to spot nearby items.

🔍

Advanced Search

Filter by category, date, location, and description keywords to narrow down results quickly and find what you're looking for in seconds.

🔔

Smart Notifications

Get instant alerts when similar items appear in the system, so you never miss a potential match. Real-time push and email notifications keep you updated.

🔒

Secure Messaging

Connect safely with finders or owners through our built-in encrypted messaging system — no personal information is shared without your consent.
